Trail Riding Mastery

Improve your skills on more challenging terrain with advanced techniques

Course Description

Our Trail Riding Mastery course is designed for intermediate riders who want to take their mountain biking skills to the next level. This intensive 3-day program focuses on technical riding, managing challenging terrain, and improving your overall confidence on the trails.

What You'll Learn

  • Advanced body positioning for technical climbs and descents
  • Cornering techniques for different trail conditions
  • Effective braking and speed control strategies
  • Navigating rocky, rooty, and steep sections with confidence
  • Basic jumping and drop techniques
  • Trail reading and line selection skills

Requirements

Participants should have at least one season of mountain biking experience and be comfortable riding blue-graded trails. You should be able to ride continuously for 2-3 hours at a moderate pace. A good level of fitness is recommended for this course.

Equipment Included

  • Full-suspension mountain bike
  • Helmet and protective gear
  • Riding gloves
  • Water bottle
